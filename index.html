Agin Hardware

family-owned for over 20 years in Dresden, Ohio

Agin Hardware is a family-owned hardware store in Dresden. Ran by Tim and Kay Agin, Agin Hardware has been in business for over 20 years, providing customer service not found at big-box stores.

"Excellent and courteous service. Good prices and selection on what you need on common and uncommon items." - Facebook review

We offer a wide range of hardware, tools, plumbing materials and electrical equipment. We also carry supplies for households, vehicles, gardening, cleaning and even some neat knick-knacks. Our selection is always affordable, we love to recommend our favorite products like Yenkin Majestic paints and if we don't have what you need, we can talk about ordering it. And for those pesky nuts and bolts you have that don't match up, we have the best nut and bolt selection around!
